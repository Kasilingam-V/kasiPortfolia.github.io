Mystic Dash

A fast-paced multiplayer obstacle course game inspired by party platformers, featuring dynamic traversal, challenging environments, and competitive gameplay built in Unreal Engine 5.

Checkpoint System

Progress is saved at checkpoints, allowing players to continue after falling.

Respawn System

Players automatically respawn at the last activated checkpoint with seamless state restoration.

Dynamic Obstacles

Implemented moving platforms, rotating hazards, and timing-based obstacle mechanics.

Gameplay Systems

Developed responsive player controls, level progression, and finish-line logic.

Project Overview

Mystic Dash is a competitive obstacle course game where players race through magical environments, avoid hazards, activate checkpoints, and reach the finish line before their opponents. The gameplay emphasizes smooth movement, responsive controls, and engaging level design.

Challenges & Solutions

Issue

Players frequently fell from obstacles, causing frustration when restarting from the beginning.

Solution

Designed a checkpoint and respawn system that records the player's latest checkpoint and restores their position instantly after falling, maintaining gameplay flow.

Issue

Obstacle timing and movement felt inconsistent during repeated gameplay.

Solution

Implemented Blueprint-driven obstacle logic using Timelines and reusable components, ensuring smooth movement and consistent gameplay behavior.

Player Experience Impact

The checkpoint system minimizes downtime after failures, while responsive movement and dynamic obstacles create a fun, competitive, and replayable platforming experience.
